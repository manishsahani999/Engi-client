Engifest'19

In February 2019, the fest will throw open its doors and welcome more than 1 ,00,000 enthusiastic students and professionals from all walks of life. ENGIFEST is a four day extravaganza; a celebration of sorts; a dense, fun-filled embodiment of the energy and conviviality which characterizes college life; a festival oozing with fun, vibrancy and entertainment. It is a product of dreams ambition and tireless hard work, a true testimony to the indomitable spirit, liveliness and sheer energy of the youth.
